Enter a comma separated list (Path) of array and object indices into the Input Medical Record text field. Once the desired data has been selected below, press the Copy Out button to copy data from the IMR to the clipboard (above). Then enter a comma separated list (Path) of array and object indices into the Ouput Medical Record text field. Once the desired destination has been selected below, press Copy In on the OMR to fill in the data in the OMR from the clipboard (above).
